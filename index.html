Multi-Account Containers CLI

The Multi-Account Containers CLI is an experimental multi-platform solution to account management, written in golang, made for devops tools. Just like its sister application (Firefox Multi-Account Containers extension), MAC-CLI lets you carve out a separate box for each of your online lives – no more opening a different browser just to open a portal in another identity!

Learn more about Multi-Account Containers in our end-user documentation.
